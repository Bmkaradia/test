OUR PHILOSOPHY

Intimacy is beautiful.

It is infinitely personal and fundamentally human – and every woman deserves to experience intimacy in the fullest possible way.

Knowledge is confidence.

When we equip ourselves with accurate information on our sexual health and well-being, and on common practices of intimacy, we are enriched as independent women, in the bedroom and beyond.

Communication is key.

When sex is considered a shameful topic or the purview of men - something not to be discussed by women - we will never be masters of our own bodies and intimate lives. Communication with each other, and with our partners, opens the door to knowledge and satisfaction.

Strength lies in numbers.

We all experience intimacy a little differently, but together, our collective experience combined can reveal the full breadth and depth of enjoying one of life’s most wonderful gifts.

Love is an investment.

To thrive as empowered mothers, to be fierce in the workforce, we invest time and energy in those roles. We leverage resources, and we connect with those who have walked the path before us. To become our best sensual selves, our intimate lives need the same sort of TLC.
